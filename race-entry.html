How to Enter

1 Check Membership

Ensure your LOS membership is active for the current season.

2 Select Race

Choose your race from the list above and click "Enter Race".

3 Enter Details

Provide your LOS number and confirm your entry category.

4 Race Day

Arrive with your timing chip and check in at registration.

Important Information

Entry Requirements
  • Valid LOS membership required
  • Working timing chip must be worn
  • Participants must be comfortable swimming the race distance
  • Wetsuits permitted in designated races
  • Under 18s require guardian consent
Race Day

Always check weather updates before race day. Races may be modified or cancelled due to conditions. Updates will be posted on our social media channels.

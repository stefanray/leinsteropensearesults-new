Frequently Asked Questions

Membership runs from January 1st to December 31st each year. New members joining mid-year will have access for the remainder of the current season.

Memberships are non-transferable and are linked to your personal timing chip number.

We welcome swimmers of all abilities. However, for safety reasons, participants should be comfortable swimming the distance of their chosen race in open water conditions.

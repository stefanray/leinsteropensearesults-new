About Us

Cold Water. Warm People.

Since 1920, Leinster Open Sea has been at the heart of open water swimming in Dublin. What started as a small group of dedicated swimmers has grown into a thriving community that welcomes everyone from first-time sea swimmers to competitive athletes.

Our History

Founded over a century ago, Leinster Open Sea has witnessed the evolution of open water swimming from a niche activity to a beloved sport embraced by thousands. Our races along Dublin's coastline have become cherished traditions, bringing together swimmers of all abilities.

Through the decades, we've maintained our commitment to fostering a welcoming community where the joy of swimming in nature takes precedence over competition alone.

Our Mission

We exist to promote and facilitate open water swimming across Leinster. Our goals include:

  • Organizing safe and well-managed open water races
  • Building a supportive community of sea swimmers
  • Encouraging participation at all skill levels
  • Preserving the traditions of Irish sea swimming
  • Connecting clubs and swimmers across the region
